Warranty

At Skade, we are committed to providing high-quality hearing aids and exceptional customer service.

Our hearing aids are covered by a warranty that protects against defects in materials and workmanship. Warranty Period: The warranty period for our hearing aids is 6-month from order date on your receipt . During this time, we will repair or replace, at our discretion, any defective hearing aids.

What Is Covered

The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship that occur during normal use of the hearing aids. It does not cover damage caused by misuse, abuse, or accidents, or normal wear and tear.

What Is Not Covered

The warranty does not cover batteries, ear molds, or other accessories. It also does not cover damage caused by exposure to extreme temperatures, moisture, or excessive sweat.

How to Make a Warranty Claim

To make a warranty claim, please contact us and provide proof of purchase, a description of the defect, and any relevant photos or videos. We will evaluate your claim and determine the best course of action.

Our Obligation

If a defect covered by the warranty is found, we will repair or replace the hearing aid free of charge. If the hearing aid cannot be repaired or replaced, we will issue a refund in the original form of payment.

Limitations

This warranty is limited to the original purchaser of the hearing aids and is not transferable. It is also subject to certain limitations and exclusions as described above.

We stand behind our products and are committed to ensuring your satisfaction. If you have any questions or concerns about our warranty, please do not hesitate to contact us by contact-us page or by email at service@skade.us.

Refund and Returns Policy

Our refund and returns policy last 30 days. If 30 days have passed since your purchase, we can’t offer you a full refund or exchange. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it. It must also be in the original packaging.

Returnable Item

The item that can be returned/refunded or exchanged within 30 days after delivery must follow the criteria as below:

    1.Faulty items damaged/broken or soiled upon arrival.
    2.Items received in the incorrect size/color.
    3. No physical damage or water damage.

Refunds

1. If your item meets the returnable criteria, please submit the following by contact-us page or by email at service@skade.us:

    – Order number
    – Product name
    – Reason for return

2. Our customer service team will respond to your request within 24-48 hours and provide you with a Return Merchandise Form (RMA) along with our return address.

3. Please return the item according to our instructions on the RMA form and send us return proof in the form of the tracking number and a valid return receipt.

4. Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. Then your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your credit card or original method of payment, within 3-5 business days.

Refunds

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next, contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted.

Exchanges

You must contact us by contact-us page or by email at service@skade.us and provide us with the order number, product name, reason for replacement, and your shipping information. It may be completed by mailing back your online return and we will ship a replacement or components to you.

Shipping returns

All shipping fees are at the customer’s expense. This includes shipping fees for a return or exchange. Shipping fees are non-refundable. If the returning is due to our reasons, the goods received are broken or not correct, please take a photo and send to us, then the consumer is not required to bear the shipping fee for this reason.

Need help?

Contact us at contact-us for questions related to refunds and returns.
