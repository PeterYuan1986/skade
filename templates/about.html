About Us

Skade Hearing is dedicated to helping the hard of hearing enjoy the sounds they love and through a team of hearing specialists provide great products at an affordable cost. We sell a wide range of products from hearing aids, batteries, wax guards, tubes, domes as well as amplified phones, and many other life-enhancing products for those with hearing loss. We strive to bring our customers leading-edge technology and hearing solutions that improve lives, relationships, and communities.

At SKADE, we are passionate about helping people with hearing loss improve their quality of life. Our mission is to provide innovative and reliable hearing aids that enhance communication and connectivity. We understand that hearing loss can be a challenging experience, which is why we are dedicated to providing compassionate and personalized care to our clients. Our team of hearing specialists has years of experience and is committed to finding the right solution for each individual's unique needs.

We offer a wide range of hearing aids from top manufacturers, including the latest digital technology. Our hearing aids are designed to meet various hearing needs and preferences, from discreet and invisible devices to advanced models with features like Bluetooth connectivity and rechargeable batteries.

At SKADE, we believe that quality service is just as important as quality products. We provide a no-pressure consultation process, where we take the time to understand our clients' needs and answer any questions they may have. We also offer ongoing support, including hearing aid maintenance, repairs, and adjustments. Our goal is to help our clients hear better and feel more connected to the world around them. We are committed to building lasting relationships with our clients and ensuring their satisfaction.

Choosing SKADE means choosing a trusted partner in your journey towards better hearing. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and discover how we can help you hear better.
